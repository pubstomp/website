044 - Cruella de Vil

10 June, 2021

This week we take a look at the origin story of Cruella! We discuss how mean Cruella actually is, and tease out the need for this origin story to exist in the first place.

Nonetheless, this is an enjoyable movie, highly recommended for the stellar acting and, of course, Emma Stone.
