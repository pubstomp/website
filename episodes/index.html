036 - Coded Bias: Machine Learning Gone Wrong

12 April, 2021
As innovation advances, regulating technology becomes a challenge. In the documentary, Coded Bias, we follow the store of Joy Buolamwini as she discovers that there’s an inherent bias in the data used to train AI algorithms. This takes us to a deeper conversation of how these algorithms are being used and where. When we think about it, we can see that these types of technology advancements can put some people at a disadvantage, further setting them apart.
